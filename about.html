About the store

Crooked Sole is not your typhical fashion and sport kicks store. Instead, Crooked Sole is owned by a guy who is only interested in selling kicks that he feels offer the best quality and design.

Nothing else matters at Crooked Sole because we understand that quality and design are not just features; they are the essence of what makes a pair of kicks extraordinary. Join us on this journey, and let your footwear speak volumes about your unique style and uncompromising standards. Welcome to Crooked Sole – where exceptional kicks are the only language we speak.

If this sounds a little elitist, it's because it is. If I don't like it, I don't sell it.

Yours truly,

Rafael Borbon(Owner of Crooked Sole)
